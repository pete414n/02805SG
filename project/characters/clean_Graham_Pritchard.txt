
'''Graham Pritchard''' (born c. [[1983]]) was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1994]]. He was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Slytherin]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].


Graham was originally a Scottish last name that meant "grey home" or "gravel homestead." Pritchard is Welsh and derived from Richard, a Germanic name meaning "brave power" and a popular name in Wales before the 15th century.
