'''Derek''' (born c. [[1982]]) was a [[Wizardkind|student]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] in [[1993]]. During his [[first year]], he stayed at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|school]] over the [[Christmas holidays]], and was surprised when [[Albus Dumbledore]] asked him whether he had had any of the [[chipolata]]s at the [[Christmas]] [[Christmas Feast|feast]]. He blushed deeply at being personally addressed by the [[Headmaster]].

==Etymology==
The name "Derek" is from a Low German form of ''Theodoric''. It was imported to England from the Low Countries in the 15th century.
