'''Mrs Dursley''' was the wife of [[Dudley Dursley]]: the couple had two<ref>[http://www.newyorker.com/reporting/2012/10/01/121001fa_fact_parker?currentPage=all After "Harry Potter", J. K. Rowling's First Novel for Adults at The New Yorker]</ref> [[Dudley Dursley's children|children]]. When her husband's cousin [[Harry Potter]] visited them with his [[Potter family|family]], their children would play while Dudley and Harry sat together quietly.<ref>[http://www.mtv.com/movies/news/articles/1572399/20071019/story.jhtml 19 October 2007 J.K. Rowling at Carnegie Hall]</ref>

She is related to the [[Dursley family]], and the [[Evans family]], who are the maternal family of her mother-in-law, [[Petunia Dursley|Petunia]]. She is related to the [[Potter family]], due to her marriage to [[Dudley Dursley]], whose cousin is [[Harry Potter]], and is related to her own maternal and paternal family.

It is unknown as to whether she knows about her husband's cousin being part of the wizarding world.
