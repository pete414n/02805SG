'''Basil''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] employee. In the [[1990s]], Basil worked for the [[Department of Magical Transportation]], most likely in the [[Portkey Office]].

At the [[Quidditch World Cup]] final in [[1994]], he was one of the [[wizardkind|wizards]] staffing the [[Portkey]] terminus, and had been there for most of the morning with a large party that was arriving from the [[Black Forest]] in [[Germany]]. He was wearing a poncho and a kilt in an attempt to look like a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]].
