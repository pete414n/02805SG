'''Mr Montague''' was the father of [[Graham Montague]]. 

==Biography==
He and his wife, [[Graham Montague's mother|Mrs Montague]], visited [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1996]] after their son was finally freed from a prolonged entrapment inside a [[Vanishing Cabinet]] in [[1996]] by [[Fred and George Weasley]]. [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]], and [[Hermione Granger]] saw them arrive at the school looking furious through the window of the [[Charms (class)|Charms]] [[Charms Classroom|classroom]].
