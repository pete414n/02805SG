'''Mr Peverell''' (fl. [[1291]]) was a [[pure-blood]]<ref name="blood"/> [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who lived during the [[13th century]], the son of [[Ignotus Peverell]]. He had a daughter, [[Iolanthe Peverell]], who inherited the [[Cloak of Invisibility]], one of the three [[Deathly Hallows]], and married [[Hardwin Potter]]. He was also an ancestor of the members of the [[Potter family]].<ref>{{WW|the-potter-family}}</ref>
