'''Gordon''' was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] who was a friend of [[Dudley Dursley]] and was a member of [[Dudley Dursley]]'s [[Dudley Dursley's gang|gang]]. In [[August]] [[1995]], after bullying [[Mark Evans]], he told his friends to come to his house the next day when [[Gordon's parents|his parents]] were out.

==Etymology==
The name "Gordon" comes from a Scottish surname which was originally derived from a place name meaning "great hill". It was originally used in honour of Charles George Gordon (1833-1885), a British general who died defending the city of Khartoum in Sudan.
