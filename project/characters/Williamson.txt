'''Williamson''' was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who worked as an [[Auror]] for the [[British Ministry of Magic]]. He was described as wearing scarlet red [[robes]] and a long pony tail when [[Harry Potter]] first met him.<ref name="OOTP36">{{OOTP|B|36}}</ref>

==Biography==
He was the first Auror to see [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] just before he fled the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] in [[1996]] after the [[Battle of the Department of Mysteries]]. Fudge then sent him and [[John Dawlish]] to deal with the captured [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="OOTP36"/>
