This "'''magic goblin'''" worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] during the [[wizardkind|wizard management]] in [[1997]]-[[1998]], after the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|fell]] to the [[Death Eaters]].<ref name="DHpt2">{{DH|F2}} - DVD 2 (''The Goblins of Gringotts'')</ref>

He was present at the bank when [[Harry Potter]], [[Hermione Granger]], [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Griphook]] hosted a [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)|break-in]] in order to retrieve one of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort's]] [[Horcrux]]es, [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]], from the [[Lestrange Vault]].<ref name="DHpt2"/>

==Behind the scenes==
*This goblin was played by [[Harrison Davis]] in {{DH|F2}}. Despite not being credited, the character is identified as "Magic Goblin" in a Behind the Scenes feature. in DVD2.<ref name="DHpt2"/>
*It's unknown why this character is called Magic goblin, as all goblins have magical abilities. Presumably, he was particularly proficient at magic compared to the average Gringotts goblin, and his job in Gringotts may have revolved around this skill.
