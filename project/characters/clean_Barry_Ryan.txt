

'''Barry Ryan''' was an Irish [[wizardkind|wizard]] who played as the [[keeper]] for the [[Irish National Quidditch team]] during the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]].


He was one of the team members that [[Harry Potter]] got to see in the [[Top Box]] during the 1994 [[Quidditch World Cup]].

After the [[1994 Quidditch World Cup|World Cup]], Ryan did a spectacular save with his boot against [[Poland]]'s top [[Chaser]], which was accidentally copied by [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]] in his [[fifth year]] at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]].

As of [[2014]], Barry Ryan did not play for the [[Irish National Quidditch team]] any more.
