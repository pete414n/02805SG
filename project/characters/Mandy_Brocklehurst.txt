'''Mandy Brocklehurst''' was a [[half-blood]]<ref name="HPM">{{HPM}}</ref> [[Wizardkind|witch]] who began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1991]]. She was in the same year as [[Harry Potter]] and was Sorted into [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]].<ref name="PS7">{{PS|B|7}}</ref>

==Etymology==
*Mandy is a variation of Amanda, which means "lovable/worth of love".
*Brocklehurst is a real surname coming from Middle English "brock" (badger) and "hyrst" (wooded hill, a favourite haunt of badgers).
*Brocklehurst was the name of the horrible school teacher in ''Jane Eyre''.

==Behind the scenes==
*The character [[Amanda]], portrayed by [[Christina Petrou]] in {{PS|FA}}, may be intended to be Mandy Brocklehurst.
*One of the clients of Cormoran Strike, a private detective and the protagonist of [[J. K. Rowling]]'s ''{{w|The Silkworm}}'', has hired him to monitor the activities of a Miss Brocklehurst.
