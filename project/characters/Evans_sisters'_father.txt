'''Mr Evans''' (died between [[1 September]] [[1971]] and [[31 October]] [[1981]]<ref name="wbd2004" /><ref name="2005interview" /><ref name="dodexplanation" />) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] and the father of [[Petunia Dursley|Petunia]] and [[Lily J. Potter|Lily Evans]]. He had two grandsons, [[Dudley Dursley]] and [[Harry Potter]], through his daughters' marriages to [[Vernon Dursley]] and [[James Potter I|James Potter]].

==Biography==
Mr Evans and [[Evans sisters' mother|his wife]] lived in [[Cokeworth]], [[England]].<ref name="cokeworth">{{WW|cokeworth}}</ref> The couple had two daughters: [[Petunia Dursley|Petunia]], born in the late [[1950s]], and [[Lily J. Potter|Lily]], born [[30 January]] [[1960]].<ref name="dh16">{{DH|B|16}}</ref><ref name="dh33">{{DH|B|33}}</ref>

The couple's elder daughter was a [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] like them.<ref name="ps4">{{PS|B|4}}</ref> Their younger daughter, on the other hand, turned out to be a [[Muggle-born]] [[wizardkind|witch]].<ref name="ps4" /> Mr Evans and his wife were proud to have a magical daughter, but Petunia thought differently, considering her sister to be a "freak."<ref name="ps4" /> According to Petunia, Mr and Mrs Evans favoured Lily, talking about her constantly.<ref name="ps4" />

On [[1 September]], [[1971]], Mr Evans and his wife took Lily and Petunia to [[Platform Nine and Three-Quarters]], in order to see Lily off to her first year at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name="dh33" />

===Death and legacy===
Mr Evans and his wife died "normal Muggle death[s]" sometime before the murder of their daughter Lily and son-in-law [[James Potter I|James Potter]], on the night of [[31 October]], [[1981]].<ref name="wbd2004" /><ref name="2005interview" /><ref name="dodexplanation" /> This meant that their grandson, [[Harry Potter]] was left in the care of Petunia, his only surviving blood relation.<ref name="ootp37">{{OOTP|B|37}}</ref>

[[File:MirrorOfErised_PM.jpg|250px|thumb|left|Harry standing before the [[Mirror of Erised]] and seeing his family for the first time]]

When Harry looked into the Mirror of Erised in [[1991]], he saw both his maternal and paternal grandparents, among a number of other relatives he had never met.<ref name="PS12">{{PS|B|12}}</ref>
