'''Connolly''' was an [[Ireland|Irish]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who played as a [[Beater]] for the [[Irish National Quidditch team]] during the [[1994]] [[Quidditch World Cup]]. The [[Broomstick]] he rode was a [[Firebolt]] which had been ordered from [[Quality Quidditch Supplies]]. He was helping support [[Aidan Lynch]] when being presented with the Cup in the top box.
