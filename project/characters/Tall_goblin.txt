This '''tall goblin''' worked at [[Gringotts Wizarding Bank]] during the [[wizardkind|wizard management]] in [[1997]]-[[1998]], after the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|fell]] to the [[Death Eaters]].

He was present at the bank when [[Harry Potter]], [[Hermione Granger]], [[Ronald Weasley]] and [[Griphook]] hosted a [[Break-in of Gringotts Wizarding Bank (1998)|break-in]] in order to retrieve one of [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort's]] [[Horcrux]]es, [[Helga Hufflepuff's Cup]], from the [[Lestrange Vault]].

==Behind the scenes==
*This goblin was played by [[Binde Johal]] in {{DH|F2}}. Despite not being credited, the character is identified as "Tall Goblin" in a Behind the Scenes feature in DVD2.
