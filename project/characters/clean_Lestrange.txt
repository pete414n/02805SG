


'''Lestrange''' (born c. [[1926]]) was a [[pure-blood]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]] and a [[Slytherin]] student at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. He was a member of [[Tom Riddle]]'s [[Tom Riddle's gang|gang]] when he attended Hogwarts, and a member of the [[Slug Club]].


 supper with his school friends [[Tom Riddle]], [[Avery (Riddle-era)|Avery]] and others]]
Lestrange attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], and was sorted into [[Slytherin]] house, eventually becoming a member of [[Professor]] [[Horace Slughorn]]'s [[Slug Club]]. He was also a member of [[Tom Riddle]]'s [[Tom Riddle's gang|gang]] when they were classmates. During his time at school, Lestrange forgot to turn in a [[Potions (class)|Potions]] essay for [[Horace Slughorn|Professor Slughorn]] and was nearly put in detention.
