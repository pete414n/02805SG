'''Mrs Montague''' was the mother of [[Graham Montague]].<ref name="OOTP30"/>

==Biography==
She and her husband, [[Graham Montague's father|Mr Montague]], visited [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1996]] after their son was finally freed from a prolonged entrapment inside the [[Vanishing Cabinet]] in [[1996]]. [[Harry Potter]], [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]], and [[Hermione Granger]] saw them arrive at the school looking furious through the window of the [[Charms (class)|Charms]] classroom.<ref name="OOTP30">{{OOTP|B|30}}</ref>
